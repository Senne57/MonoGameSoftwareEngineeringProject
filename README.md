# MonoGameSoftwareEngineeringProject
## Tutorial
### Controls
- Q/Left Arrow = Move Left
- D/Right Arrow = Move Right
- Z/Up Arrow = Jump
- E = Attack
- R = Respawn (only when dead)
### Normal enemy
The normal enemy can be killed by the normal attack (E) or by jumping on the head of the enemy.
### Amored enemy
The amored enemy can only be killed with the normal attack (E) if you try and jump on his head you will take damage instead.
### Cannon enemy
the cannon enemy shoots cannonballs which you have to dodge if the cannonbal hits you it wil explode and do more damage to the player then the normal/armored enemy will (the cannon cannot be killed or destroyed).
### Spike trap
The spike trap is small so be carefull and look out to not step on it or the player will take more damage then the normal/armored enemy will wil deal. (the spike trap cannot be destroyed).
### Boss
The boss is a flying demon that Chases you till it catches you. if it catches you you instantly die, the only way to kill it is to dodge the cannonballs from the cannons and let the cannonballs hit the boss.
## Win condition
### Level 1
Kill all enemies to proceed to the next level.
### Level 2 
Kill all enemies while dodging the cannonballs and spike traps to proceed to the next level.
### Level 3
Run away from the boss while dodging the cannonballs so the boss dies from the cannonballs to win the game.
